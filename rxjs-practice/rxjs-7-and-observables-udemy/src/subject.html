Observable - subject

Subject can be described as a hot observable. Subject itself is a shared source of the Notification. we can have multiple subscriptions to the same subject and once we call next on this subject, this next notification will be multicast to all the subscribers.
When we click on emit no action will be shown.
First click on subscribe and then enter A click on emit to see the subject in action.
second click on subscribe and then enter B click on emit to see the subject in action.
